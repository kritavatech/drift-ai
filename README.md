# Drift AI

Drift is an experimental AI-powered behavioral trajectory visualization concept designed to explore how habits, routines, consistency patterns, and decision-making behaviors may influence possible future outcomes over time.

## Core Concepts

* Behavioral trajectory analysis
* Future-path simulation
* Adaptive AI reasoning
* Parallel outcome visualization
* Self-reflection systems
* Human-centered AI experiences

## Vision

To create AI experiences focused on behavioral understanding and trajectory awareness rather than traditional productivity workflows.

## Status

Currently in MVP and concept exploration phase.
